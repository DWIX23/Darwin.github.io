College of Science Building and its Facilities

The College of Science building at the University of Rizal System Morong Campus is a state-of-the-art facility designed to provide students and faculty with a conducive learning and research environment. The building features modern classrooms, laboratories, and lecture halls equipped with the latest technologies and equipment. The college also has a dedicated computer laboratory and library that provide students with access to the latest scientific and academic resources. The building has spacious lounges and study areas where students can interact with their peers and faculty. Overall, the College of Science building and its facilities are designed to provide students with an immersive and engaging learning experience that prepares them for the challenges and opportunities of the modern world.

Building and its Facilities

College of Science

Laboratory (Biology)

A chemical laboratory of a college of science is a specialized facility designed for students and faculty to conduct experiments and research related to chemistry. It typically contains a variety of equipment and materials needed for conducting chemical experiments, such as fume hoods, analytical balances, spectrometers, and glassware. The laboratory is used for teaching undergraduate and graduate courses in chemistry, as well as for conducting research in areas such as organic chemistry, biochemistry, and materials science. Safety is of utmost importance in a chemical laboratory, and strict protocols are followed to ensure the safe handling and disposal of chemicals and other hazardous materials.

Computer Laboratory (COS)

The Computer Laboratory of the College of Science is a dedicated facility within a college or university that provides resources, infrastructure, and support for computer-related research, teaching, and learning activities. It may include computing equipment such as servers, workstations, and specialized hardware, as well as software, data storage, and networking resources. The laboratory may also offer technical support, training, and consultation services to faculty, students, and staff. The Computer Laboratory of the College of Science is designed to facilitate advanced research and education in computer science, data science, and related fields, and to promote innovation and collaboration in these areas.

Student Center (COS)

A student center- is a central hub on a college or university campus that offers a range of services and spaces to enhance the student experience. It provides social areas, dining options, study spots, administrative offices, event spaces, and more. Student centers foster community, support academic and social needs, and contribute to a well-rounded campus environment.

Math Laboratory (COS)

A math laboratory in a university is a dedicated space or facility where students can engage in hands-on exploration, experimentation, and collaborative learning related to mathematical concepts. It often provides access to specialized software, tools, and resources to help students deepen their understanding of mathematical principles through practical activities and problem-solving exercises.

Gym

The Gym of University of Rizal System Morong Campus is a large indoor facility designed for sports and fitness activities. It serves as a hub for various athletic programs and events organized by the university, including basketball, volleyball, badminton, and other indoor sports. The gym is equipped with standard sports equipment, such as basketball hoops, volleyball nets, and gym mats. It also has a seating capacity for spectators and a locker room for athletes. Overall, the gym provides a conducive environment for physical activity and promotes a healthy lifestyle among students, faculty, and staff of the University of Rizal System Morong Campus.

Library

The Library of University of Rizal System Morong Campus is a facility located within the campus of the University of Rizal System in Morong, Rizal, Philippines, that provides a wide range of resources and services to support the research, teaching, and learning needs of the university community. It houses a vast collection of books, journals, multimedia materials, and online databases, which are carefully selected and organized to cater to the academic and scholarly interests of students, faculty, and researchers in various fields of study. The library also offers various services, such as reference and research assistance, information literacy instruction, interlibrary loan, and document delivery, among others, to help users access and use information effectively.
