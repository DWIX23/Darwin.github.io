BS Math

The BS Mathematics program is a degree program that provides students with a strong foundation in mathematical theory and application. The program covers a range of topics such as calculus, algebra, statistics, and differential equations. Graduates of the program can pursue careers in fields such as finance, education, engineering, computer science, and other related areas.

The Bachelor of Science in Mathematics (BSMath) is a four-year degree program designed to give students a background in mathematical modeling, scientific computing, and various modern applications of mathematics. The program covers major areas in pure mathematics exposure to mathematical applications in statistics, operation research, and actuarial science. It also teaches students the proper use of problem-solving approaches to investigate and understand mathematical content and analyze mathematical statements and construct solutions.
